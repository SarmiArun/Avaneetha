Quality

Continual innovation through continuous research and development makes us strong in quality. Various stringent tests are conducted on every product we manufacture or procure to ensure quality and consistency. Our team of qualified professionals conducts extensive research to produce superior quality fabrics.

Selecting raw materials

Avaneetha uses high-quality raw cotton, such as Shankar6, which offers superior comfort and fitness.
Re-engineering of the products and processes has proven to be successful in reducing costs and optimizing material consumption.
Even for small quantities of product, the plant's product lines are designed to change the product with minimum changeover losses.

Process Measuring

At every stage of manufacturing, quality parameters are monitored and ensured by quality testing equipment like the HVI, the Uster Tester (UT-5), and the Classimat.
We are using quality cotton, plant, and machinery, as well as process testing, to achieve the top Uster 5 level.

Chemical Free Yarn

In most cases, we do not detect contamination levels higher than 2 per kg of knitted fabrics.
As a result of our blow room contamination sorter and yarn clearer, all of our yarns are automatically leveled, coned, and spliced.
We continually leverage our core competence to learn, adapt, and deliver faster to achieve our quality objectives.
Our chemical-free fabric made from our yarns has contemporary handles and feels.

Starting with its facility in Coimbatore, Avaneetha, since its inception, has grown into one of the largest producers of 100% cotton yarns in India.
