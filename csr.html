CSR

Corporate Social Responsibility

For the development of our nation, rural community development is of paramount importance in providing quality education, proper healthcare, sanitation, efficient waste management, etc. CSR policy at Avaneetha Textiles emphasizes achieving a healthy balance between profits and contributing to society.

Education

We empower organizations and nonprofits to leverage their social impact through measurable, meaningful corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives. We aim to inspire the desire for learning and knowledge at every stage through Formal schools, Quality Primary education, Girl Child Education, and Undergraduate and Postgraduate programs.

Healthcare

Avaneetha Textiles is committed to social responsibility by setting up hospitals, primary health care centers, and nursing homes, providing medical equipment to hospitals, and promoting preventive health through awareness programs and medical camps. In addition to providing high-quality healthcare solutions and devices to our customers, we also provide exceptional attention to people, profit, and the environment. Together, let's make sustainability a reality.

Sustainable Livelihood

Through Security Academy, Self-Help Groups for Women Empowerment, Vocational Training, Partnerships with Industrial Training Institutes, and Agriculture Development and Better Farmers Focus, Avaneetha Textiles generates sustainable livelihood opportunities in the agricultural sector. We aim to enhance family incomes in periphery villages by building their capacities in sustainable livelihoods.

Infrastructure Development

Throughout its history, Avaneetha Textiles has continuously worked towards rural development. We strive to establish essential infrastructure facilities, housing facilities, safe drinking water, sanitation & hygiene, and renewable energy sources that form the foundation of sustainable development. To maintain the long-term sustainable development of society, the Company prioritizes and undertakes activities from time to time.

Windmill

We are committed to social responsibility through our company's mission by generating energy from windmills. Our social responsibility demands that we pay back Mother Nature for all that she has provided us with. A windmill with a capacity of 5.6 MW generates power for us.

Whistle Blower Policy

In maintaining high standards of professionalism, honesty, integrity, and ethics, Avaneetha Textiles is committed to conducting business in a transparent and fair manner. The "Whistle Blower Policy" was established to provide our employees a safe and fearless working environment.

Starting with its facility in Coimbatore, Avaneetha, since its inception, has grown into one of the largest producers of 100% cotton yarns in India.
