Towards our goal of being one of the largest producers of 100% cotton yarns in India, our team of qualified professionals constantly conduct extensive research to produce superior quality fabrics. Our products are enhanced with the latest technology and techniques and are recognized worldwide.

Knitting Division

Our Knitting machines are of brands Pailung and Mayer & Cie make.
We also do a fully fledged grey knitted fabric production.
We convert all type of knit construction, Viz, Single jersey, Interlock, Rib, Pique flaw and other varieties.
We also use auto striper machines for yarn dye fabric, and flat bed collar knitting machines for full integration in-house.
All machines are maintained with Spandex attachments.
We also execute 100% inspection of fabric rolls under 4 Points Inspection System.
Our machine specifications are flexible – Dia from 20" to 36" -24/28GG

At Avaneetha Textiles, we also have a knitting division with a capacity of delivering 1,00,000 meteres of knitted fabric per year, producing Grey Single Jersey, Interlock and Rib fabrics in diameter ranging from 20″ to 36″ meteres. We also produce processed and dyed fabrics in large variants.

Starting with its facility in Coimbatore, Avaneetha, since its inception, has grown into one of the largest producers of 100% cotton yarns in India.
